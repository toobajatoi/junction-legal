Privacy Policy — Junction

Last updated: May 24, 2026

App: Junction (com.junction.app)

Contact: toobajatoi44@gmail.com

Junction ("we", "our", "the app") is a UX flow puzzle game. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

### Account information

When you sign up or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address (email sign-up or OAuth provider)
  • Display name you choose in the app
  • Authentication identifiers from Google or Apple Sign-In

### Gameplay data

We store progress related to your account, including:

  • Levels completed and scores
  • XP, coins, streaks, and preferences
  • Online Blitz rating, wins, and losses (if you use online mode)
  • Daily challenge progress

### Purchases

If you buy subscriptions or in-app products:

  • We receive entitlement status (what you unlocked) through RevenueCat and the app store (Google Play or Apple App Store)
  • We do not collect or store your full payment card number — billing is handled by Google or Apple

### Device / technical data

  • A guest device identifier (stored locally) if you play without an account
  • Standard app logs and crash data from our hosting providers (if enabled)

We do not sell your personal information.

How we use information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save and sync game progress
  • Match you with opponents in Online Blitz
  • Unlock premium content you purchase
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

Third-party services

Junction uses:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and backend APIs (supabase.com/privacy)
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase and subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Google Play Billing — Android purchases (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Apple In-App Purchase — iOS purchases (apple.com/legal/privacy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Apple Sign-In — optional sign-in on iOS (apple.com/legal/privacy)
  • Expo / EAS — app builds and updates (expo.dev/privacy)

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We keep account and gameplay data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us at toobajatoi44@gmail.com. We may retain minimal records where required by law.

Children

Junction is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact toobajatoi44@gmail.com to make a request.

To manage or cancel a subscription, use your Google Play or App Store subscription settings.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
